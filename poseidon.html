People want to share and have their voice heard. But...

Current social media constrains information.

Information is clustered in friends, followers, and close proximities.

               
There are 2 problems:

  1. Exposure

    People's voices are limited to these clusters, and unless they are Kim Kardashian, their voice is bounded.
    It is hard for people to reach new audiences.

  2. Impact

    People donâ€™t know where their voice has spread and how their voice has traveled.

Our solution is Ripple
a platform that:

  1. Spreads information beyond any boundary

    Expose content beyond any friend, follower, or radius restrictions.

  2. Visualizes influence

    Show how far content travels, where it spreads to, and who it impacts.

  3. Surfaces engaging content

    Allow engaging content from around the world to grow exponentially.

How it works:

  1. Start a "ripple", or post. It goes to people nearby.

  2. Those who receive a ripple can spread it to others near them, or dismiss it.

  3. Ripples travel to more people as users continue to spread them.

Our Vision

The easiest way to

Ripple changes the way information spreads

Information on Ripple travels freely.



Content previously stuck in clusters now travel so long as they are relevant.

Information relevant to a city will reach users in that area, while globally appealing content can reach every Ripple user.

It allows you to reach new audiences across the world

People and brands alike can reach new audiences around the world more easily, without a friend or follower system.

It allows users to understand their impact

Likes, retweets, upvotes, etc are 1-dimensional measures of engagement.

Each Ripple visualizes how many times the content was spread, where it has traveled to, and how it got there.

Users love interacting with these ripple maps to understand how content reached them.
