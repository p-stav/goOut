People want to share

We have a natural want to talk about ourselves and have people listen.

Current social media constrains information

Information is clustered in friends, followers, and close proximities.

               
There are 2 problems:

  • Exposure

    Peoples' voices are limited to these clusters, and unless they are Kim Kardashian, their voice is bounded.

Our vision is to be the easiest way to

Share your story     &     explore your world


  • Broadcast your opinions beyond your circles
  • Visualize the impact you have

  • Interact with people outside your circles
  • See the most engaging content being shared from around the world



How it works:

  1. Start a "ripple", or post. It goes to people nearby.

  2. Those who receive a ripple can spread it to others near them, or dismiss it.

  3. Ripples travel to more people as they continue to spread
So what?

Ripples are boundless

Ripple removes boundaries in how content travels. Information is not stuck in clusters. It simply diffueses through a system:

Ripple for those seeking exposure. Connecting with people across the world is captivating and adventurous; it's like travelling.

Ripple propagates the most engaging content

Users spread engaging ripples to people across the globe, while noise does not travel far. Ripples that reach many people are vetted by ripplers and are very likely to be engaging.

Ripple visualizes your impact

Likes, retweets, upvotes, etc are 1-dimensional measures of engagement.

Ripple visualizes how many times something was spread, where each ripple has gone, and how it got there.

Users love interacting with these ripple maps to understand how content reached them.
