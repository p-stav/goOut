About Us


We all love going out.

And frankly, you deserve to find the best places to dance, grab drinks, and catch-up with friends.

However, venues have different atmospheres that constantly change. But what if you knew what each bar and club were like right now? Looking for a place with #LustyIntentions, a place to #DanceYourFaceOff, or somewhere that's #EasyLikeSundayMorning? Let's find it.

So get out there and have a blast tagging places to let our team know each place's vibe. Then, use Kefi to find your ideal scene.


That's why we called this app "Kefi"; it means "fun" in Hebrew and "the spirit of joy" in Greek.


Bring the Kefi tonight,

Gal and Paul.
