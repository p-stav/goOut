About Us


We want you to find the best places to dance, drink and catch up with friends.

The places we go to are constantly changing. The club you where you like to DanceYourFaceOff might be Crickets tonight. The bar that usually serves WeakDrinks might have a new GenerousBartender.

So use Kefi to find a place that matches your mood for tonight and then get your friends to join.


That's why we called this app "Kefi"; it means "fun" in Hebrew and "the spirit of joy" in Greek.


Bring the Kefi tonight,

Gal and Paul.
