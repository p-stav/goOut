About Us

We want you to find the best places to dance, grab drinks, and catch up with friends when you decide to go out.


The places we go to have constantly changing atmospheres. The club you like to #DanceYourFaceOff might be #Crickets tonight. That bar that usually serves #WeakDrinks might have a new #GenerousBartender. Or perhaps you're looking for a place with #LustyIntentions or #EasyLikeSundayMorning. Let's find it.


So use Kefi to find a place that matches your mood for tonight, then get out there and have a blast sharing your thoughts about it.


That's why we called this app "Kefi"; it means "fun" in Hebrew and "the spirit of joy" in Greek.
