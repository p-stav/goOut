September Competition
#MyWorld

What's your world like? Is it populated by ancient trees and colorful critters, or do massive buildings dominate your skyline? Do you like to watch watercolor sunsets, or would you rather go bungee jumping in Australia? Share your world with the #MyWorld tag this September.



How to participate:

  1. Get Ripple on the iOS or Google Play Store

  2. Post Ripples with #MyWorld in your caption
    You will receive 1 point per spread on your top #MyWorld Ripple.

  3. Refer your friends
    You will receive 1 point per friend that downloads Ripple with your username as their referral code.

  4. Gain followers
    You will receive 1 point per follower that you have at the end of the competition; however, you will lose points for posts asking people to follow you! We encourage you to gain followers by consistently posting unique and original content.








The top 3 users at the end of the competition (October 1st) will be rewarded!

1st Place: $50 gift card of your choice and a feature in the next Ripple blog post
2nd Place: $25 gift card of your choice and a feature in the next Ripple blog post
3rd Place: $10 gift card of your choice and a feature in the next Ripple blog post
