Privacy Statement

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Ripple (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by Kefi Labs LLC. The Application allows users to post content and send it to fellow users nearby.

USER PROVIDED INFO

The Application obtains the information you provide when you download and register the Application. Registration with us is optional. However, please keep in mind that you will not be able to use core functionality of the Application.

When you register with Kefi Labs LLC and use the Application, you provide (a) a username, password, and email; and (b) transaction-related information, such as the different posts you create and posts you choose to spread to others.

In addition, the Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile devices unique device ID, the Application version, and information about the way you use the Application.

such as when you create a game, post a comment, or take a picture of someone in the Application, and (c) other information such as Device ID and access time. Take note that the Application requires your access to your Facebook account, which you provide to (a) provide your name, (b) your friends who have also installed the application, and (c) your profile picture.

We may also use the information you provided us to contact you from time to time to provide you with important information, required notices and marketing promotions.

LOCATION INFORMATION

When you visit the mobile application, we use GPS technology (or other similar technology) to roughly determine your current location in order to determine your general vicinity. We obstruct your current location by a 500 meter radius to protect you. We utilize this data to send your Ripples to users near you, as well as send you nearby Ripples from other users. We will not share your current location with other partners or corporate entities.

Ripple makes use of technology which updates your location once you have travelled than 500 meters. Ripple also monitors location changes while in the background. To change this behavior, you should toggle off the option to always use location service, and choose the one which allows for location services while using the application in your mobile phone system settings.
