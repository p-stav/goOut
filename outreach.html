We see 2 problems in social media

  1. Exposure

    It's hard to reach new audiences.
    People and businesses alike want to promote thoughts, work, and events. Their voices are bounded in clusters of their friends, followers, or close proximity.

                   

  2. Impact

    It's hard to gauge the impact content has.
    There is no good way to see real-time, multi-geography feedback on content.

Our soultion is Ripple
A platform that surfaces engaging content & visualizes how it spreads

How it works:


  1. Start a "ripple", or post. It first goes to people nearby.

  2. Those who receive a ripple can spread it to others near them, or dismiss it.

  3. Ripples travel to more people as users continue to spread them.
Ripple maps how content spreads and allows users to comment.

Our Vision

Ripple is the easiest way to reach new audiences & explore engaging content

Ripple changes the way information spreads.
Information on Ripple travels freely; content previously restricted within clusters now diffuses through our system so long as it is relevant.


Ripples travel so far and long as they are engaging and relevant.
Reach new audiences
Ripple's spreading mechanism makes it easier for quality content to reach new audiences.

  • Consumers and brands no longer need to amass a friend/follower system to make impressions. One can download Ripple and immediately broadcast a message. It's the easiest way to share your story.
  • Ripples travel to more people with different background, thoughts, and views. understand the value to exposing their brand to larger audiences. Ripple will be a great tool for them.
  • Our future will allow people to share content with users who have self-selected for their interests. People and brands alike can reach these audiences.
Allows users to understand their impact
Likes, retweets, upvotes, etc are 1-dimensional measures of engagement. Each Ripple visualizes how many times it was spread, where it has traveled to, and how it got there.

  • Consumers love interacting with these ripple maps to see how content reached them and how far their messages travel.
  • Businesses can determine the effectiveness of their brand, analyze the success for different geographical markets across the world, and learn more about the users which spread their information.
Explore the world around you
Ripple's goal is to be both a local and global tool. Content travels so far and so long as Ripplers find them engaging and relevant. Posts relevant to a city will reach users in that area, while globally appealing content will travel further.

  • Local exploration
    Ripple will also function as a local news and events platform. Content relevant only to a specific area can reach many users there without travelling beyond those boundaries. We are well positioned to offer users deals to local retailers.
  • Global trends
    Users will see the best content from around the world. They will aslo be able to pan over a map to any location on Earth and find real-time analysis of trending ripples and current events.

Ripple has 3 types of users

Artists + photographers    

These users are artists who ripple their own work.

They are photographers, bloggers, and painters/sculptors who are looking for exposure and validation.

Users who share their viewpoints    

These users ripple thoughts and viewpoints on current events. They want to spark a conversation.

Users who share spontaneous moments    

These users ripple images showcasing their in-the-moment experiences. These include scenic views, pets, and other things they come across.
