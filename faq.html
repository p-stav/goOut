What is Ripple?

Ripple is a geolocation based social app where people can reach others beyond any boundary or social cluster. Users post, or start a "ripple," and others continue to spread the posts if they like it. Eventually, the the best ripples travel to people around the world, and users can see where each ripple travels. For more information, refer to the about us page.


What is the idea behind Ripple?

Our goal is to provide people a way to gain more exposure without going through the hardships of attracting the right communities of people. On Ripple, there is no 'like' or 'favorite' button. As long as content is relevant, it spreads to people on a global scale.


How much does Ripple cost?

Ripple is free and is available on the Apple app store and Google Play store.


What happens when I swipe a ripple?

Swiping right spreads the ripple to people closest to you, wherever you are.
Swiping left dismisses the ripple, meaning the content will not spread to anybody.


How do I post?

For iOS users, click the pencil button centered on the navigation bar at the bottom of the screen.
For android users, click the pencil button on the bottom right corner of the screen.


How does the point system work?

Ripple has a point system that helps you increase your Ripple reach. As you gain points and level up, more people around you receive posts that you start or spread. You gain points by spreading or dismissing ripples, and for each spread on a post you start.


What ripples show up in the trending feed?

The most spread ripples started within the last five days appear on the trending feed.


Does Ripple show my exact location?

Ripple does not disclose your exact location when you post or spread a Ripple. We randomize your location within a 1/3 radius. Visit the privacy page for full information.


I don't want others to be using my photos for their own use.

Ripple does not give your content or information to advertisers without your consent, or provide the license for others to use your content. Ripple does retain the license to use your content for featuring purposes on our website, social media, or blog posts. Please inform us if you do not wish to be featured.


I saw a ripple that offends me. Can I flag it?

There is an option to report content on each ripple. The ripple will be removed if deemed inappropriate.


Is Ripple compatible with other services?

Currently, Ripple can be connected with Twitter. We will be adding more compatible services very soon.
