Our Mission

Our goal is to make Ripple the service people use to share their latest joke, an upcoming local event, or something others near them would find engaging.


Our mission is to find the niches of people that will first realize Ripple’s potential and stickyness. Once we have found those groups, we will need to feed the the word-of-mouth, viral growth engine that would follow.

What We Need

Below are the list of items that Ripple needs help with.
These tasks will require a solid grasp in development, data analytics, marketing, and/or strategy.


  1. Developer

    Our code base is written in Objective-C/Swift, Java/Android SDK, Javascript, and Python (for scripting purposes)
    As we discover new ways that we can provide value to users, we will need client changes. There are also always bugs that we need to fix and improve.

    We are looking for someone to drive client testing, bug fixing, and the development of new features.

  2. Niche Discovery

    We need someone to drive the discovery of groups of people that will first realize Ripple’s potential.

    This includes marketing Ripple to more people, hearing feedback on the product from these users, and engage in guerilla growth tactics to increase awareness for these niches.

  3. Ripple Campaigns

    One way to attract attention is to run competitions, host upcoming conferences/events/meetups, etc. We need to raise awareness by running these types of campaigns.

    Ripple is looking to drive several campaigns to raise awareness and help determine what tactics work best for user acquisition and engagement.

  4. Hypothesis Testing

    We need to experiment with our algorithms and content to find what our userbase best responds to. Do they prefer to be flooded with ripples? How much does a lack of ripples effect our engagement levels? Is there significant activity increase if we have a close clustering of users?

    Ripple is looing to have someone drive these hypotheses, determine what the best environment is to maximize our engagement and user levels, and make sure that we recreate that environment every day.

  5. Power User Engagment and Outreach

    At this early stage in a company, it is imperative that we keep our best customers happy and engaged. They will be the ones that will start the viral chain reaction of telling friends to download Ripple.

    We need someone to drive user outreach, power user feedback sessions, and strategy on how to put each power user in a position to promote Ripple.
