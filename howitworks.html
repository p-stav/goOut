How it works

Start a ripple that is sent to people geographically near you.

These people can spread a ripple to more users near them.

Ripples travel so far and long as people spread them.
Only the most interesting ripples travel across the world.

Earn points to increase your reach, or the number of people
your content travels to whenever you spread or start a ripple.
